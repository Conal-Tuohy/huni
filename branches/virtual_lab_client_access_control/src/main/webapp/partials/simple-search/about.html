What is HuNI?

The Humanities Networked Infrastructure (HuNI) is a national 'virtual laboratory' project that is being developed as part of the Australian government’s NeCTAR (National e-Research Collaboration Tools and Resources) program.

HuNI is combining information from 28 of Australia’s most significant cultural datasets. These datasets comprise more than 2 million authoritative records relating to the people, objects and events that make up the country’s rich heritage. HuNI is also deploying an integrated suite of software tools that enable researchers to work with this large-scale aggregation of Linked Data.

It is being developed as a partnership between 13 public institutions, led by Deaking University.

HuNI will transform the future of research methods in the humanities and creative arts.
